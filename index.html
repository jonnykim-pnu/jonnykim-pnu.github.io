Jonny Jungyun Kim

I’m a linguist specializing in experimental phonetics. I am Associate Professor at the Pusan National University. My research focuses on sociophonetic variation, speech perception, and the interaction between phonetic detail and social meaning.

I am also known by the name 김정윤 in Korea.
